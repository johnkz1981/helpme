## Bookmarks
 *
 * Windows/Linux:
 *   F11 Toggle Bookmark
 *   Ctrl+F11 Toggle Numbered Bookmark (Bookmark with Mnemonic)
 *   Shift+F11 Show bookmarks
 *   Ctrl+0..9 Navigate to numbered bookmark
 *
 * macOS:
 *   F3 Toggle Bookmark
 *   Alt+F3 Toggle Numbered Bookmark (Bookmark with Mnemonic)
 *   Command+F3 Show bookmarks
 *   Ctrl+0..9 Navigate to numbered bookmark
